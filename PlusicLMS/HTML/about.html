Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate educators and technologists, PlasicLMS began with a simple yet powerful vision: to break down barriers to quality education and create a platform where anyone, anywhere can learn, grow, and succeed.

What started as a small startup with just three courses has now transformed into a global learning platform serving over 50,000 students across 120+ countries. Our journey has been fueled by the success stories of our learners and the dedication of our world-class instructors.

Md. Nayemur Rahman
Founder & CEO
